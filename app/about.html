How we started ?

It all started when we tried to scratch our own itch, we realize that there isn't any good software available for Real Estate Investors in the market, most of the existing products either dont work or requires a lot of cusotmer support to make it work.

We still see a number of new investors, who are still saving their call logs, followup tasks on Excelsheets, we tried all of that, only then we realize that we've to provide that a software thats affordable and reliable, and most importantly saves time, so that investors who want to pursue their dreams on part time should be able to do it.
